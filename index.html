Interesting Facts About Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing sin colour is a important part of the communication among Chameleons
  • Most Chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts About Camel

  1. Camel's ears are furry
  2. Camel can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet
  3. When they find water, they drink as much as possible

Interesting Facts About Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animal
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts About Panda

  1. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim
  2. Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit
  3. The giant panda’s scientific name is Ailuropoda melanoleuca, which means “black and white cat-foot”

Interesting Facts About Cicada

  • There are around 3,000 cicada species, according to National Geographic
  • Cicadas can be black, brown or green and can have red, white or blue eyes.
  • Cicadas do not sting or bite, according to the University of Michigan

Interesting Facts About TeddyBear

  1. The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.
  2. Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902
  3. The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’
